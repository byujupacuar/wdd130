History

Torrent Trail was founded in the early 2000s by a group of adventure lovers who turned their passion for whitewater rafting into a business. Named after the roaring rapids from their first journey, the company is known for blending thrilling river expeditions with a commitment to environmental conservation. Over the years, Torrent Trail has gained a reputation for skilled guides, safety, and unforgettable experiences, welcoming everyone from beginners to experienced rafters.
